A World Painted: Exploring the Global Tapestry of Murals

Murals, large-scale paintings applied directly to walls, have adorned civilizations for centuries. From ancient Egyptian tombs to the vibrant street art of modern cities, murals have served as a powerful medium for storytelling, cultural expression, and social commentary. In recent years, a unique phenomenon has emerged: the Murals World Map, a digital resource that catalogues and showcases murals from around the globe. This map serves as a vibrant testament to the universality of art and the enduring power of murals to connect people across cultures and continents.
