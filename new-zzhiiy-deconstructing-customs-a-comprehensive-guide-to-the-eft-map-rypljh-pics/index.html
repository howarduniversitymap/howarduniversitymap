Deconstructing Customs: A Comprehensive Guide to the EFT Map

The map known as "Customs" in Escape from Tarkov (EFT) serves as a quintessential introduction to the game’s mechanics and atmosphere. This sprawling, realistic depiction of a fictionalized Russian port facility offers players a diverse range of environments to explore, objectives to complete, and adversaries to encounter. Understanding the intricacies of Customs is crucial for navigating the game’s early stages and developing essential skills for success in the more challenging maps that lie ahead.

Navigating the Terrain: An Overview of Customs

Customs is a large map, encompassing a variety of distinct locations, each with its own unique characteristics and strategic considerations.

  • The Docks: This expansive area features multiple container stacks, warehouses, and a large, open cargo yard. It’s a prime location for long-range engagements and flanking maneuvers.
  • The Dorms: A multi-story building complex offering a variety of interior spaces, from apartments to a large, central hallway. It’s a hotspot for close-quarters combat and loot acquisition.
  • The Old Gas Station: A dilapidated structure with a large, open area suitable for ambushes and defensive positions.
  • The Checkpoint: A heavily guarded area with a large, open space that serves as a chokepoint for players entering or exiting the map.
  • The Power Station: A large industrial building with a unique layout and multiple access points. It’s a popular destination for players seeking high-value loot.
  • The Junkyard: A sprawling area filled with scrap metal and abandoned vehicles, offering opportunities for concealment and flanking maneuvers.
  • The Village: A small residential area with houses, a school, and a church. It’s a good location for finding loot and setting up ambushes.

Understanding the Objectives: Loot, Extraction, and Survival

The primary objective in EFT is to survive and extract with loot. Customs offers a variety of ways to achieve this, depending on the player’s chosen approach.

  • Loot Acquisition: Players can find valuable items throughout the map, ranging from weapons and armor to medical supplies and valuable keys. The Dorms, Power Station, and warehouses are known for their high-value loot.
  • Extraction Points: There are multiple extraction points on Customs, each with its own unique characteristics and risks. These points can be found near the docks, the checkpoint, and the village.
  • Survival: The most crucial objective in EFT is to survive the encounter. Players must be aware of their surroundings, utilize cover effectively, and engage in strategic combat.

Strategic Considerations: Mastering the Art of Customs

Effective gameplay on Customs requires a nuanced understanding of the map’s layout, the behavior of other players, and the optimal strategies for achieving objectives.

  • Movement and Positioning: Players should prioritize safe movement and strategic positioning, utilizing cover effectively to avoid detection and engage in advantageous firefights.
  • Loot Prioritization: Time is a valuable resource in EFT, so players should prioritize acquiring high-value loot and avoid spending excessive time on low-value items.
  • Awareness and Communication: Players should maintain constant awareness of their surroundings, communicate with teammates effectively, and anticipate potential threats.

Navigating the Challenges: Overcoming the Obstacles

Customs presents a variety of challenges for players, ranging from intense firefights to navigating complex environments.

  • Enemy Encounters: Players will encounter other players, AI-controlled Scavs, and even hostile PMCs (Private Military Companies) on Customs. Managing these encounters effectively is critical for survival.
  • Environmental Hazards: The map features various environmental hazards, such as open areas, long distances, and potential chokepoints, which can significantly impact gameplay.
  • Time Pressure: Players must manage their time effectively, balancing loot acquisition with extraction before the timer expires.

The Importance of Understanding Customs

Mastering Customs is a crucial step in progressing through EFT. It provides players with the foundational skills and knowledge necessary for success in the game’s more challenging maps.

  • Developing Fundamental Skills: Customs allows players to develop essential skills such as movement, positioning, combat, and loot acquisition.
  • Understanding Map Dynamics: The map’s diverse environments and strategic considerations offer players a deep understanding of the game’s mechanics and objectives.
  • Building Confidence and Experience: The relative accessibility of Customs allows players to gain confidence and experience in EFT’s core gameplay loop.
