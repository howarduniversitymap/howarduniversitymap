A Glimpse into St. Michaels’ History

St. Michaels, founded in 1664, has a rich history intertwined with the Chesapeake Bay. Its early settlers, primarily English and Dutch, established a thriving maritime community, with shipbuilding and oystering becoming the backbone of its economy. The town’s strategic location on the Bay made it a crucial center for trade and transportation, attracting merchants and travelers from far and wide.
